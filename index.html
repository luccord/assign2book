Please join us for Iqaluit's annual book fair, hosted for your convenience at the Iqaluit Centennial Library. Join travelers from around the world and experience our incredible yearly event this March. Enjoy the city's many sights, beautiful weather and of course purchase a book or two!

Our City

Iqaluit has much to offer. While you're here feel free to check out the sights, and enjoy our breathtaking architecture. During the Book Fair, there will be many other exciting activities available across the city, so take advantage and enjoy your trip here to the fullest! Some interesting things to do include snowshoeing, snowmobiling, and enjoying our traditional Inuit snowcones. Delicious!

The Library

The Iqaluit Centennial Library is the territory's state of the art facility and was purposefully chosen to host this year's important annual Book Fair. With its stunning modern design, it has served the community for many years and continues to be an integral space for learning and the hosting of other special events.

Nunavut

Nunavut is Canada's hidden northern gem, reknowned for their incredible landscape, people, amenities, and of course--its remarkable, and internationally recognzied annual Book Fair. Please view this incredible short video to become aquainted with our beautiful territory! Highlights include a curated compilation of acclaimed works by Canadian authors, and a Collectors Corner with deluxe and rare printings that are perfect for display.
