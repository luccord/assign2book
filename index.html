The Iqaluit Book Fair

The North Reads

March 7-8, 2023 - The Iqaluit Centennial Library - 220 Sinaa, Iqaluit, NU X0A 0H0

Please accompany us for Iqaluit's annual book fair, hosted for your convenience at the Iqaluit Centennial Library. Join travelers from around the world and experience this incredible yearly event this March. Enjoy the city's many sights, beautiful weather and of course purchase a book or two!
